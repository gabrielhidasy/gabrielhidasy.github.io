Fetuccini Al Limone

This is a classic recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g Fetuccinne

  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil

  • 30g Butter

  • 200ml Heavy Cream

  • 100ml Milk

  • Juice of two lemons

  • Nutmeg

  • 1 Onion

  • Spring onion

  • Lemon zest from your lemons

  • Salt and Pepper to taste

How to cook

  1. Cook pasta in salty as the sea water

  2. Whisk the cream and the lime juice till combined

  3. Brown onion with oil and butter.

  4. Add cream mixture to the onions.

  5. Add nutmeg to and fix salt and pepper.

  6. Reduce sauce.

  7. Add pasta to sauce and cook for 2-3 min.

  8. Serve with lemon zest and spring onion

Notes

  • Works with penne.

  • If the sauce gets too reduced, milk can bring it back.

  • Pasta water can also work.

  • I made half a measure and it was too much lemon, or maybe it was the MSG
